Honey Garlic Chicken Noodles

Get Ready to Dive In!

This high protein, low fat meal is guarenteed to satisfy your taste buds! Using chicken thighs, with a combination of garlic honey, sriracha, and spices give a sweet and spicy flavor that will keep you coming back for more.

The egg noodles are the perfect complement to the chicken thighs. Bathed in a honey/sriracha/soy sauce combination add some sweet heat with umami.

Ingredients

Chicken

  • 800 grams Boneless Skinless Chicken Thighs
  • 4 garlic Colves minced
  • 15 grams Honey
  • 1 tbsp Paprika
  • 1 tbsp Black Pepper
  • 15-20 mL of Sriracha

Noodles and Sauce

  • 220 grams of Egg Noodles
  • 70 mL of Low Sodium Soy Sauce
  • 10 grams of Sesame Oil
  • 10 grams of Honey
  • 10 grams of Sriracha
  • 20 mL of Water

Optional Ingredients

  • Green onion
  • Sesame Seeds

Steps

  1. Place chicken thighs into bowl and combine ingredients for marinade
  2. Heat a pan on medium-high heat, and cook chicken thighs for 5 mins on each side (until internal temperature reaches 165 degrees)
  3. Remove chicken from pan and set aside (We will use the pan later for the noodles)
  4. Prepare noodles according to package
  5. While noodles are cooking, combine ingredients for the sauce in a bowl
  6. Once noodles are finished, add the sauce and mix until noodles are coated
  7. Garnish with green onion and sesame seeds
